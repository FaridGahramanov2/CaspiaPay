# CaspiaPay — Payment Infrastructure for Azerbaijan's Digital Economy

---

## The Problem

- Azerbaijan's digital economy is growing rapidly, but payment integration remains complex
- Developers spend weeks integrating with existing gateways
- Small and medium merchants are underserved — too small for direct bank integration, too complex for existing solutions
- No platform supports modern use cases: marketplace payments, subscriptions, split payments

---

## The Solution

**CaspiaPay is a developer-first payment platform for Azerbaijan.**

- Any business can accept Visa/Mastercard payments through a simple API
- Integration takes **15 minutes**, not weeks
- Built-in support for marketplaces, subscriptions, and split payments
- Modern developer experience (think Stripe, but for Azerbaijan)

---

## How It Works

CaspiaPay operates as a **payment facilitator** under the bank's acquiring license:

1. **Bank provides:** Card processing infrastructure, settlement, regulatory compliance
2. **CaspiaPay provides:** Developer tools, merchant onboarding, dashboard, fraud detection, technical support

All card transactions flow through the bank's existing infrastructure. CaspiaPay handles everything on top.

---

## Revenue Model

**Merchants pay:** 2.5-2.9% per transaction

- CaspiaPay retains the margin
